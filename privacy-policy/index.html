Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Introduction

Welcome to the website of On Purpose Young Adults (onpurposeya.com), a youth ministry of the Calhoun Seventh-day Adventist Church. We value your privacy and are committed to keeping this online space safe, transparent, and completely free of invasive data collection.

This policy outlines our practices regarding data collection. Simply put: we do not collect, track, or sell your personal data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Unlike most websites, we do not require you to sign up, log in, or provide personal details to view our site.

  • No Contact Forms: We do not host contact forms or newsletter signup boxes on our domain.
  • No Cookies: We do not write or use cookies to track your browsing behavior.
  • No First-Party Tracking: We do not implement Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking tags.

2. Third-Party Links & Services

Our website acts as a hub to connect you with our events and community. In doing so, we display links to external third-party tools:

  • RSVP Forms (Google Forms & Tally.so): When you click RSVP to register for an event, you are redirected to an external Google Form or Tally form. Any personal details you submit (such as name, email, or phone number) are collected directly by those third-party services and are stored securely in our church's administrative account.
  • Location Maps (Google Maps): We link to Google Maps to help you find our physical meeting locations. Google may track your location or use its own cookies once you visit their service.
  • Donations (AdventistGiving): Clicking donate redirects you to the official church donation platform. We do not process, receive, or store any financial or payment information.

3. Email Communication

If you choose to contact us directly via the email addresses listed on our website (like info@onpurposeya.com), we will only use your email address and any shared message content to answer your questions or assist you. We will never share or sell your email address.

4. Contact Us
